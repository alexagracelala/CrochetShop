About Me

My name is Alexa Grace LaFontaine-Larson, and I am a 19-year-old girl from Woodbury, Minnesota. I have two 30-year-old sisters who are twins, and a little brother named Landon who just turned 10! I am a freshman at Lewis University where I am studying Computer Science. I love to read, play video games, thrift, and, of course, crochet. In the picture is my lovely boyfriend and my brother.

My Crochet Journey

I first started to learn how to crochet with my mother when I was 11-years-old. We went to classes at our local Joann's Fabric to learn both crocheting and knitting. Crocheting definitely stuck more than knitting, and I have had an on-again, off-again relationship with crocheting throughout my life. Going to college away from home reignited my desire and passion for Crocheting. I have gotten to a level I never once was and enjoy it more than ever.
